Classic Pancakes

These classic pancakes are fluffy, golden, and easy to make for a perfect breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and stir until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour batter and cook until golden on both sides.
